# 研究倫理聲明 / Research Ethics Statement

## 中文聲明

本研究儲存庫所含之資料係蒐集自一個 Discord 遊戲公會於 2026 年 1 月之文字對話紀錄。資料蒐集與處理過程嚴格遵守下列學術倫理規範：

### 1. 知情同意原則（Informed Consent）

- 於資料蒐集啟動前，研究者已向該公會管理者及所有參與成員充分說明本研究之**目的、流程、資料使用方式、潛在風險及匿名保護措施**
- 所有參與者均於完整理解後給予**正式同意**，方納入研究資料範圍
- 未取得同意之公會成員，其對話內容及與研究參與者之延伸對話均已於資料篩選階段予以排除
同意書空白範本及簽署摘要請見 [docs/consent_summary.md](docs/consent_summary.md)。

### 2. 去識別化處理（De-identification）

本資料集已執行下列去識別化措施：

- **成員身份代號化**：所有成員以匿名代號 P1–P21、R1 表示，原始 Discord 帳號、暱稱、使用者 ID 均未保留
- **內容掃除**：對話文本中可能涉及第三方識別資訊之內容（如他人姓名、其他平台帳號、真實位置等）已進行替換或移除
- **平台資訊隱蔽**：本資料集未揭露伺服器名稱、公會名稱、相關遊戲名稱等可間接識別社群之資訊
- **時間戳粗化**：對話時間僅保留至日期層級，未保留具體時分秒，以降低交叉識別風險

### 3. 研究者雙重身份揭露（Dual-role Disclosure）

研究者本人為該公會之長期成員，於本研究進行期間亦持續參與該社群之日常互動。此雙重身份已於：

- **資料蒐集前**：向所有參與者明確揭露
- **論文撰寫中**：於第三章 3.4 節及第四章 4.4.4 節進行反身性檢視（reflexivity）
- **本儲存庫**：以代號 R1 與其他參與者採一致之去識別化處理方式

### 4. 資料用途限制

本資料集**僅供學術研究用途**，使用者承諾：

- 不得用於商業目的
- 不得試圖透過任何技術手段重新識別資料中之個人身份
- 不得將資料轉售或散布予未遵守相同倫理規範之第三方
- 若基於本資料進行衍生研究，須於相關發表中適當引用本研究

### 5. 資料保存與存取

- 本儲存庫採用 CC BY 4.0 授權，公開供學術社群存取
- 原始（未去識別化）資料保存於研究者本人之加密儲存裝置，**不對外公開**
- 若有特殊學術用途需求，請透過 Issues 頁面或 email 聯繫研究者協商

## English Statement

This repository contains de-identified textual interaction data collected from a Discord gaming guild during January 2026. The data collection and processing adhered strictly to the following ethical standards:

### 1. Informed Consent

- Prior to data collection, the researcher fully informed the guild administrator and all participating members of the study's **purpose, procedures, data usage, potential risks, and anonymization measures**
- All participants provided **formal consent** after thoroughly understanding the study
- Conversations from non-consenting guild members, as well as related conversations involving them, were excluded during the data filtering stage

### 2. De-identification

The following de-identification procedures were applied:

- **Member anonymization**: All members are represented by anonymous codes (P1–P21, R1); no original Discord usernames or user IDs are retained
- **Content sanitization**: Third-party identifying information within conversations (e.g., real names, accounts on other platforms, geographical locations) has been replaced or removed
- **Platform anonymization**: The server name, guild name, and relevant game name are not disclosed in this dataset
- **Timestamp generalization**: Timestamps are retained only at the date level, with no specific hour/minute/second information, to reduce cross-identification risks

### 3. Disclosure of Researcher's Dual Role

The researcher is a long-term member of the studied guild and continued to participate in daily interactions during the research period. This dual role has been:

- **Pre-disclosed**: Explicitly communicated to all participants prior to data collection
- **Documented in the thesis**: Reflexive examination conducted in Section 3.4 and Section 4.4.4
- **Anonymized in this repository**: The researcher is represented as R1, with the same de-identification protocols as other participants

### 4. Usage Restrictions

This dataset is **for academic research purposes only**. Users agree to:

- Refrain from any commercial use
- Make no attempt to re-identify individuals through any technical means
- Not resell or distribute the data to third parties who do not commit to the same ethical standards
- Properly cite this research in any derivative work

### 5. Data Storage and Access

- This repository is publicly accessible under the CC BY 4.0 license for the academic community
- The original (non-de-identified) data is stored on the researcher's encrypted personal device and is **not publicly available**
- For special academic use cases, please contact the researcher via Issues or email
